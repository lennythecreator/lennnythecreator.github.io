Who am I?

As a coder, I am a purveyor of creation and innovation. From the days of tinkering with my family's antiquated computers, coding has been an ever-burning passion within me. As a Nigerian immigrant, I came to the United States with a firm belief that the pursuit of better opportunities is a right, and that my time on this earth should be spent making a lasting impact. I am a firm believer that success is not a matter of chance, but rather a result of unyielding willpower and unshakable determination. And with every keystroke, I am ready to prove it.
